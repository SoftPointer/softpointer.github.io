  2. Submission

    Event Forcing: Consistent Autoregressive Multi-Event Video Generation via Event-Level Test-Time Correction

    Peiyuan Zhu, Shaoan Xie, Wen Tian, Zijian Li, Guangyi Chen, Kun Zhang

    Under submission, 2026.

    "Event Forcing studies autoregressive multi-event video generation under accumulated temporal errors and event-level inconsistency.     Peiyuan Zhu
